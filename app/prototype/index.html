FAQ

Q: How much do you charge?

A: I hate to answer a question this way, but in all honesty it varies depending on the project. Please feel free to contact me and give me a general description of the problem and I should be able to narrow it down for you.

Q: Do you charge per hour or per project?

A: While I prefer to charge per hour, I understand that this is not always possible. So, I can also do per project price. If we do negotiate a project price, a clear scope will need to be defined along with deliverables and anything that falls outside of that scope can be discussed.

Q: What if the project I have in mind requires a team?

A: Luckily I've had the opportunity to work with some other very talented developers, designers, testers, and business analysts over the years. Many of them also do full-time or part-time contract work and always love new challenges. If we decide that additional resources are needed I'll bring them on board. This tends to work well since these are people who I know to be skilled and are excellent to work with.

Q: Are you insured?

A: Of course! I have errors and omissions insurance and can provide any documentation you may require.

Q: How are payments handled?

A: Arrangements are flexible depending on the type of the project, but you will be sent a detailed invoice(s) no matter what the type of work being done. I can then accept payment through check, bank transfers, or most major credit cards.

Q: Can I talk to a current or previous client about your work?

A: Absolutely! Feel free to contact me and I will put you in touch with one or several clients.

Q: So are you good?

A: YES!
